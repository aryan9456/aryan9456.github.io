About Me
Web developer | Programmer

I am Chloe Green, a hockey fan, hustler, audiophile, international Swiss style practitioner, and independent graphic designer. I operate at the crossroads of beauty and sustainability to express ideas through design and act at the crossroads of beauty and elegance to create great work for living breathing human beings. I work with fortune 30 companies and startups.

I have graduated from an art college in 2013, ready to start my professional career as an artist. It was not the easiest thing, but I was full of excitement and belief in myself. The struggle of some months resulted in getting my dream job in a well-known IT company.

It was like a dream come true for me, and I put all of my enthusiasm into the work I did. I became the senior graphic designer of the company I was working for. It was a huge responsibility, and I tried to do my best. Working in this position has helped me gain more experience.
